NFL teams

The National Football League (NFL) is a major American professional football organization consisting of 32 teams, split evenly between the AFC (American Football Conference) and the NFC (National Football Conference). It is one of the four makor professional sports leauges in the US and is considered the highest level of proffesional football in the world.

The leauge was founded in 1920 as the American Professional Football Association (APFA) and was renamed the National Leauge (NFL) in 1922. The season culminates in the Super Bowl, one of the biggest sporting events globally, where the AFC and NFC champions compete for the league championship.

Seasons

NFL players usually play during the NFL season, which runs from september to February

  • Games are typically played on Sundays, Mondays, and Thursdays.
